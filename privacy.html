1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how our app ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our service that integrates with Instagram's Basic Display API.

2. Information We Collect

Our app only accesses publicly available information from Instagram through the Basic Display API. We do not collect or store any private or non-public information.

3. Use of Information

The public information we access is used solely for the purpose of providing our app's services to you. We do not store this information beyond what is necessary for the immediate functioning of the app.

4. Data Storage

We do not maintain any database or long-term storage of information accessed through Instagram's API. All data displayed in our app is retrieved in real-time from Instagram's servers and is not retained by us.

5. Third-Party Services

Our app uses Instagram's Basic Display API. Please refer to Instagram's Privacy Policy for information on how they handle your data.
