User Data Deletion

Note: Our app does not store any user data beyond what is publicly available through Instagram's Basic Display API. We do not maintain any database of user information.

Instagram Data

As we use Instagram's Basic Display API, the data we access is controlled by Instagram. To manage or delete your Instagram data:

  1. Go to your Instagram account settings
  2. Select "Privacy and Security"
  3. Scroll to "Data Download" and select "Request Download"
  4. To delete your account and data, go to the Instagram Delete Your Account page

App-Specific Data

Our app does not store any user-specific data. When you use our app:

  • We only access publicly available information through Instagram's API
  • We do not store this information beyond the immediate use in the app
  • No user data is retained after you close the app

Revoking App Permissions

If you wish to revoke our app's access to your Instagram data:

  1. Go to your Instagram account settings
  2. Select "Apps and Websites"
  3. Find our app in the list and select "Remove"
